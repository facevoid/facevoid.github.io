Hi, I'm Saurav, a Machine Learning Researcher at Luber Lab, where I recently completed my PhD under Dr. Jacob Luber. My research focuses on generative models in medical imaging. I'm working on AI models that use multimodal approaches to help improve cancer diagnostics and explore personalized treatment options, with applications in virtual staining, multiplexed imaging, and computational pathology. My work is published in NEJM AI, IEEE, and Springer venues.
